Frequently Asked Questions

How do I study for the TSSA G2 exam?
Our comprehensive G2 exam guide provides detailed study strategies and our premium practice exam simulator with 500+ questions and detailed explanations. Combined with our Code Compass tool (free for Ontario Regulations and CSA B149.1-25/B149.2-25 Sections 1-4), you'll have comprehensive preparation for your Gas Technician 2 certification.
What's the best free HVAC calculator for Canadian standards?
Our HVAC Pro Tools app includes professional-grade calculators specifically designed for Canadian HVAC work, including heat load calculations with Canadian climate data, refrigeration P-T charts for all common refrigerants, and unit converters for imperial/metric systems.
Are there free tools for TSSA G3 exam preparation?
Yes! Our complete free G3 study guide includes practice exams, chapter review questions for all 9 units, and access to our Code Compass tool. All G3 resources are completely free to support Canadian gas technician students.
Are these tools suitable for HVAC students and educators?
Absolutely! All our tools are designed for students, working technicians, and educators. They're used in Canadian HVAC programs and are completely free for educational use. Our training modules align with Canadian gas technician certification requirements.
How do I prepare for the Canadian gas code exam?
Use our Code Compass tool to search and study Canadian gas installation regulations (free for Ontario Regulations and CSA B149.1-25/B149.2-25 Sections 1-4, premium subscription for full code access), combined with our comprehensive chapter review questions covering all units from safety protocols to advanced piping systems and appliance installation.
What makes these HVAC tools different from others?
Our tools are built by experienced HVAC professionals and educators specifically for the Canadian market. They include Canadian climate data, CSA standards, TSSA regulations, and are designed based on 20+ years of field experience and 10 years of teaching HVAC programs.
