⚠️ Important Educational Disclaimer

CSA Code References

All study materials and resources resources provided by LARK Labs reference the following current Canadian standards:

  • CSA B149.1-25: Natural Gas and Propane Installation Code (2025 edition)
  • CSA B149.2-25: Propane Storage and Handling Code (2025 edition)
  • Ontario Regulation 215/01: Fuel Industry Certification and Resource
  • TSSA Act: Technical Standards and Safety Act (Ontario)

Our content is designed to support exam preparation and code compliance understanding. Always consult official CSA publications and current regulations for authoritative information.

Educational Purpose

LARK Labs provides supplementary educational resources to help students and professionals prepare for TSSA certification exams. Our materials are CSA-compliant (meaning they align with current CSA standards) but are not official CSA resources materials. We recommend using our resources alongside official resources programs and CSA publications.

No Affiliation

LARK Labs is an independent educational content creator and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to CSA Group, Technical Standards and Safety Authority (TSSA), or any regulatory body. Our resources are created by experienced HVAC professionals and educators to support the gas technician community.

For official certification requirements, always refer to TSSA.org and official CSA publications available through CSA Group.
