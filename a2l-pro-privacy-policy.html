Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority
A2L Pro is designed with privacy in mind. All calculations are performed locally on your device with no data transmission to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

A2L Pro is a completely offline application that performs all calculations locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or calculation data.

Data We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Device location or GPS data
  • Calculation inputs or results
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information or identifiers

2. How We Use Information

Since A2L Pro does not collect any user data, there is no information for us to use, share, or process. All refrigerant calculations and system diagnostics are performed entirely on your device without any network connectivity requirements.

3. Data Storage and Security

Any app preferences or settings you configure are stored locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to LARKLabs or any third parties.

Local Storage Only:

  • App settings and preferences remain on your device
  • Calculation history (if any) is stored locally
  • No cloud storage or external data transmission
  • Data is protected by your device's security measures

4. Third-Party Services

A2L Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates completely independently without external dependencies.

5. App Permissions

A2L Pro requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Internet Access: Not required - app functions completely offline
  • Storage: Only for local app data and preferences
  • Camera/Microphone: Not requested or used
  • Location: Not requested or used

6. Children's Privacy

A2L Pro is a professional HVAC tool intended for use by qualified technicians and professionals. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13, as our app does not collect any user data whatsoever.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect any user data, there is no information to share, sell, or disclose to third parties. Your calculations and usage of the app remain completely private to you.

8. International Users

A2L Pro can be used worldwide without any privacy concerns, as no data is collected or transmitted regardless of your location. All processing occurs locally on your device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Professional Use

A2L Pro is designed for professional HVAC technicians working with A2L refrigerant systems. Users should have proper training and certification for A2L refrigerant handling. Always follow local codes and manufacturer guidelines.

A2L Safety Reminder: A2L refrigerants are mildly flammable. Always follow proper safety procedures, use appropriate leak detection equipment, and comply with local regulations when working with A2L systems.
