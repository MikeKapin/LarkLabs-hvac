üìñ What This Unit Covers

Gas Trade Unit 13 focuses on control systems and automation:

  • Chapter 1: Fundamentals of Controls - Control types (thermostats, limit switches, safety controls, pressure switches), sensing elements, and operating principles
  • Chapter 2: Control Circuits - Sequencing logic, ladder diagrams, control wiring, and integrated control systems
  • Chapter 3: Servicing and Troubleshooting - Diagnostic procedures, control testing, fault diagnosis, and repair techniques

üéØ TSSA G2 Exam Relevance

CSA Code Reference: CSA B149.1-25 Section 7 (Appliance Controls and Safety Devices)

Exam Coverage: Control systems appear in approximately 18-22% of G2 exam questions. Understanding control logic and troubleshooting is essential for intermediate-level certification.

üí° Study Tips for Unit 13

  • Learn control types: thermostats, limit switches, safety controls, and their applications
  • Master sequencing logic and understand control circuit operation
  • Practice troubleshooting control failures using systematic diagnostic procedures
  • Study integrated control systems and electronic control boards
  • Understand the relationship between controls and overall appliance safety
