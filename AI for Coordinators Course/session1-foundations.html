0:30-1:30 Compare AI Assistants: Your First Tasks

Goal: Try the same task in all 3 AI tools to see which one fits your work style best.

1 Task 1: Draft an Email (10 minutes)

Copy this exact prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, AND Gemini:

Write a friendly email to faculty about our upcoming curriculum review meeting. Details: Thursday, March 20, 2pm, Room 305. We'll discuss: gen ed requirements, new course proposals, and assessment updates. Keep it under 150 words and include a clear subject line.

Steps:

  1. Paste this prompt into ChatGPT first
  2. Copy the result to a document
  3. Paste the same prompt into Claude
  4. Copy Claude's result
  5. Finally, try it in Gemini
  6. Compare: Which email would you actually send?

2 Task 2: Create a Meeting Agenda (10 minutes)

Now try this prompt in all three tools:

Create a 1-hour meeting agenda for our Program Advisory Board. Include: welcome, industry trends discussion, curriculum updates, student success metrics, and action items. Add time allocations for each section.

Notice the differences:

  • Which AI gives the best formatting?
  • Which includes the most practical details?
  • Which would save you the most time?

3 Task 3: Simplify Complex Text (10 minutes)

Let's test how each AI handles simplification:

Rewrite this for students in simple, friendly language: "Prerequisites must be satisfied prior to enrollment. Students failing to meet prerequisite requirements will be administratively withdrawn. Prerequisite overrides require department chair approval and submission of form ADM-227." Make it encouraging and clear.

1:30-2:00 Create a One-Pager with Gamma

Quick Presentation: "5 Ways We Support Student Success"

Follow these exact steps:

  1. Go to gamma.app and sign in
  2. Click "Create new" ‚Üí "Generate"
  3. Choose "One-pager" format
  4. Paste this prompt:
Create a one-page visual guide titled "5 Ways We Support Student Success" Include: Academic Advising, Tutoring Center, Career Services, Mental Health Resources, and Financial Aid Counseling. Make it colorful and encouraging. Target audience: new students.
  1. Click "Generate" and wait 30 seconds
  2. Choose a color theme you like
  3. Click "Export" ‚Üí "PDF" to save
Success! You just created a professional handout in under 3 minutes. This would normally take 30-45 minutes in PowerPoint.
