Seal and Insulate

A well-sealed home can dramatically improve your HVAC system's efficiency. Make sure windows, doors, and ductwork are properly sealed. Adding insulation in key areas can prevent heat loss. This simple step can cut energy bills by up to 20%.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

While optimizing your HVAC system, be wary of common pitfalls:

  • Ignoring drafty areas: Even small drafts can lead to significant heat loss.
  • Over-relying on heat pumps in extreme cold: Supplement with a furnace if necessary.
  • Neglecting maintenance: Skipping regular check-ups can lead to costly repairs.
