Furnace Maintenance and Troubleshooting

A well-functioning furnace is essential for staying warm. Here's how you can ensure your furnace is in top shape:

  • Inspect the furnace filter and replace it if necessary, as a clogged filter can reduce efficiency.
  • Check the pilot light and flame sensor; these should be cleaned regularly to maintain optimal performance.
  • If you notice strange noises or inconsistent heating, it may be time to call a professional for a thorough inspection.

For more detailed guidance, visit our training resources.

Boosting Energy Efficiency

Improving energy efficiency not only reduces costs but also enhances comfort. Consider these strategies:

  • Seal leaks in ductwork to prevent heat loss.
  • Install a programmable thermostat to better control heating schedules.
  • Upgrade to energy-efficient models if your system is outdated.

Heat Pump Technology and Performance

Heat pumps are gaining popularity as an efficient heating solution. Ensure yours is ready for winter:

  • Clear any debris around the outdoor unit to ensure proper airflow.
  • Check for ice accumulation and defrost as needed.
  • Regularly schedule performance checks to maintain efficiency.
