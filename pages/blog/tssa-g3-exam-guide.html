Understanding the TSSA G3 Certification

What is the G3 License?

The Gas Technician 3 (G3) license is your entry point into the gas fitting profession in Ontario. This certification allows you to:

  • Install and service gas appliances up to 100,000 BTU/hr input
  • Work on residential gas systems and appliances
  • Perform basic gas piping and connections
  • Begin building experience toward G2 certification

G3 Exam Requirements

To take the TSSA G3 exam, you must:

  • Complete an approved gas technician training program
  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Pass a pre-examination medical (if required)
  • Pay the examination fee ($100 as of 2025)

üí° Why G3 is Your Perfect Starting Point

The G3 certification provides excellent earning potential while you gain experience. Many technicians work successfully for years with just a G3 license, and it's the required foundation for advancing to G2 and G1 certifications.

G3 Exam Format and Content

The TSSA G3 exam consists of 100 multiple-choice questions covering 9 main units:

  • Unit 1: Safety protocols and regulations (15%)
  • Unit 2: Tools and equipment (10%)
  • Unit 3: Gas properties and combustion (12%)
  • Unit 4: Codes and regulations (18%)
  • Unit 5: Basic electricity (10%)
  • Unit 6: Technical drawings and manuals (8%)
  • Unit 7: Customer relations (7%)
  • Unit 8: Gas piping systems (10%)
  • Unit 9: Gas appliances (10%)

Day of Exam Success Tips

What to Bring

  • Two pieces of government-issued photo ID
  • Clean copy of CSA B149.1 (no notes or highlighting)
  • Non-programmable calculator
  • Basic drawing tools (ruler, pencil)

Exam Day Strategy

  • Read questions carefully: Watch for keywords like "NOT," "EXCEPT," "ALWAYS"
  • Manage your time: Aim for 1.8 minutes per question
  • Use elimination: Rule out obviously wrong answers first
  • Trust your preparation: Your first instinct is usually correct
  • Review if time permits: Check questions you marked for review
