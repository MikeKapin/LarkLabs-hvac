Gas Trade Unit 16: Gas Absorption Refrigeration

TSSA G2 Certification - Practice Questions & Chapter Reviews

📋 Certification Level: TSSA G2 (Gas Technician 2 - Intermediate)
⚠️ Prerequisites: G3 Certification + 2 Years (4,000 hours) Experience

Master Gas Trade Unit 16 with free CSA B149.1-25 compliant practice questions for the TSSA G2 certification exam. This unit covers gas absorption refrigeration systems, including operation principles, installation procedures, and maintenance requirements for gas-fired refrigerators.

📖 What This Unit Covers

Gas Trade Unit 16 focuses on gas absorption refrigeration fundamentals:

  • Chapter 1: Operation - Absorption cycle principles and flame characteristics
  • Chapter 2: Installation Procedures - Leveling, venting, and connection requirements
  • Chapter 3: Maintenance and Servicing - Troubleshooting and repair procedures

🎯 TSSA G2 Exam Relevance

CSA Code Reference: CSA B149.1-25 Section 9 (Refrigeration Appliances)

Exam Coverage: Gas refrigeration topics appear in approximately 8-12% of G2 exam questions.

💡 Study Tips for Unit 16

  • Understand absorption refrigeration cycle operation principles
  • Master flame characteristics and burner adjustments for refrigerators
  • Learn proper leveling requirements and their critical importance
  • Study troubleshooting procedures for refrigeration issues
  • Review safety control functions and testing procedures
