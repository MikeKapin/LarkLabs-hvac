🛡️ Safe Trading Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to ensure safe and successful tool exchanges

👥
Meet in Public

Always meet in well-lit, public locations. Many police stations offer safe exchange zones.

🔍
Inspect Before Buying

Test tools thoroughly and check for safety recalls or damage before completing purchase.

💳
Use Secure Payment

Prefer cash or secure payment methods. Avoid wire transfers or cryptocurrency.

📋
Verify Seller Identity

Check seller ratings, certifications, and ask for identification when meeting.
