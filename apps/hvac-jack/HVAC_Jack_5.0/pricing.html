Frequently Asked Questions

How do text queries and photo analyses work?
Text queries are any questions you ask HVAC Jack. Photo analyses involve uploading images of equipment for AI-powered visual diagnostics. Both are counted separately against your monthly limits.
What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?
You'll be prompted to upgrade to a higher tier to continue using HVAC Jack. Your usage resets at the start of each billing cycle.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
Yes! You can change your subscription tier at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.
How does the Business plan work with multiple users?
The Business plan includes 5 user seats that share a single quota pool of 1,000 text queries and 250 photo analyses per month. Perfect for small HVAC teams.
Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?
All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime from your account settings.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe payment processor.
