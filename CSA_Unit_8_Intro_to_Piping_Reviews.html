Gas Trade Unit 8: Introduction to Piping

TSSA G3 Certification - Practice Questions & Chapter Reviews

📋 Certification Level: TSSA G3 (Gas Technician 3 - Basic)

Master Gas Trade Unit 8 with free CSA B149.1-25 compliant practice questions for the TSSA G3 certification exam. This unit covers gas piping fundamentals including materials, sizing, connections, and support requirements per CSA B149.1-25 Section 4.

📖 What This Unit Covers

Gas Trade Unit 8 introduces essential gas piping system fundamentals:

  • Chapter 1: Piping Materials - Steel, copper, CSST, and approved piping materials
  • Chapter 2: Pipe Sizing Basics - Understanding gas load and basic sizing principles
  • Chapter 3: Piping Connections - Threading, welding, and approved joining methods
  • Chapter 4: Piping Supports and Installation - Hangers, supports, and installation requirements

🎯 TSSA G3 Exam Relevance

CSA Code Reference: CSA B149.1-25 Section 4 (Gas Piping Systems)

Exam Coverage: Gas piping fundamentals appear in approximately 15-18% of G3 exam questions, covering materials, installation, and basic sizing concepts.

💡 Study Tips for Unit 8

  • Learn the approved piping materials and their applications per CSA B149.1-25
  • Understand basic pipe sizing principles and gas load calculations
  • Know proper threading, joining, and connection methods for different materials
  • Review piping support spacing and installation requirements
  • Study CSA B149.1-25 Section 4 requirements for piping installations
