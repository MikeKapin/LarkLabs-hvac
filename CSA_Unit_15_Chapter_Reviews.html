This unit covers domestic gas appliances including ranges, clothes dryers, barbecues, and lamps with appliance-specific code requirements, installation clearances, venting, and troubleshooting.

üìñ What This Unit Covers

Gas Trade Unit 15 focuses on specific domestic appliance requirements:

  • Chapter 1: Ranges - Gas range installation, manifold pressure adjustment, burner adjustment, flexible connectors, and oven controls
  • Chapter 2: Clothes Dryers - Dryer installation, venting requirements, exhaust duct sizing, controls, and troubleshooting
  • Chapter 3: Barbecues - BBQ installation, hose connections, burner maintenance, ignition systems, and seasonal inspection
  • Chapter 4: Lamps - Gas lamp types, mantle replacement, ventilation requirements, and maintenance procedures

üéØ TSSA G2 Exam Relevance

CSA Code Reference: CSA B149.1-25 Section 9 (Specific Appliance Requirements)

Exam Coverage: Domestic appliances appear in approximately 12-16% of G2 exam questions. Understanding appliance-specific codes and installation requirements is essential for certification.

üí° Study Tips for Unit 15

  • Learn appliance-specific code requirements for each appliance type
  • Master installation clearances and protection requirements
  • Study venting requirements specific to each appliance (especially dryer venting)
  • Practice troubleshooting common appliance issues and component failures
  • Understand flexible connector limitations and proper installation techniques
