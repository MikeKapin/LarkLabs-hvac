üìù Step-by-Step Setup Guide

Create Your ChatGPT Account

  • Visit chat.openai.com
  • Click "Sign up" and use your work email
  • Verify your email address
  • Choose the free plan (sufficient for this course)
  • Test it: Type "Explain what a program coordinator does" and press Enter

Set Up Claude

  • Go to claude.ai
  • Sign up with your email or Google account
  • Verify your phone number (required for security)
  • Test it: Ask "What's the difference between you and ChatGPT?"

Create Google Gemini Access

  • Visit gemini.google.com
  • Sign in with your Google account (or create one)
  • Accept the terms of service
  • Test it: Try "Search the web for recent AI education trends"

Register for Gamma

  • Navigate to gamma.app
  • Sign up with your email (free tier includes AI features)
  • Complete the quick tutorial
  • Test it: Click "Create new" to see the interface

Organize Your Workspace

  • Bookmark all four tools in your browser
  • Create a folder called "AI Tools" in your bookmarks
  • Consider pinning the tabs if you use them frequently
  • Save your passwords in a password manager

üí° Pro Tips for Getting Started

  • Use descriptive names: When creating accounts, use professional profiles that match your work
  • Enable two-factor authentication: Add extra security to protect your accounts
  • Explore mobile apps: ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini all have excellent mobile apps
  • Start simple: You don't need to understand everything immediately - learning is iterative!
  • Keep a learning journal: Document discoveries and useful prompts you find
