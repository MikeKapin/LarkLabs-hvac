Put Your New Skills to Work!

Time to apply everything you've learned in Session 1. Complete these hands-on challenges to solidify your understanding of AI tools for coordinators.

üéØ Session Goals

  • Complete at least 3 practical challenges
  • Reflect on your learning experience

üèÜ Practical Challenges

Choose at least 3 challenges to complete. Start with the easier ones and work your way up!

Easy

Challenge 1: Email Draft Challenge

Task: Use ChatGPT to draft 3 different types of emails:

  • A welcome email for new students
  • A reminder about an upcoming deadline
  • A thank you note to department heads

Deliverable: Save your best email draft and note which AI you used and why.

Easy

Challenge 2: AI Comparison Report

Task: Ask the same question to ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini:

"What are 5 ways program coordinators can use AI to save time?"

  • Compare the responses
  • Note differences in style, depth, and suggestions
  • Choose which answer you prefer and explain why

Deliverable: A short comparison (can use your course notes feature).

Medium

Challenge 3: Create a Program One-Pager

Task: Use Gamma to create a professional one-pager for one of your programs or services:

  • Include program overview, benefits, and contact info
  • Customize the colors to match your institution
  • Add your department logo
  • Export as PDF

Deliverable: A polished one-pager ready to share with colleagues.

Medium

Challenge 4: Video Announcement

Task: Create a 30-60 second video using Pictory announcing an upcoming event or deadline:

  • Write a clear, engaging script
  • Let Pictory generate the video
  • Add AI voiceover or background music
  • Ensure captions are enabled

Deliverable: An exported video ready for social media or email.

Hard

Challenge 5: Complete Communication Package

Task: Create a full promotional package for a workshop or event using multiple AI tools:

  • Draft announcement email (ChatGPT/Claude)
  • Create visual flyer (Gamma)
  • Design promotional video (Pictory)
  • Write social media posts for 3 platforms (ChatGPT)

Deliverable: A coordinated set of materials ready to launch.

Hard

Challenge 6: AI-Powered Workflow

Task: Identify a recurring task in your work and create an AI-assisted workflow:

  • Document your current process (step-by-step)
  • Identify which steps AI can help with
  • Test the AI-assisted workflow
  • Measure time saved
  • Document the new process with AI prompts to use

Deliverable: A documented workflow you can share with colleagues.
