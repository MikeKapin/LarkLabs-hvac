üí° Pro Tips for Better Videos

  • Keep It Short: 30-90 seconds is ideal for social media
  • Hook First: Start with your most compelling message
  • One Scene = One Idea: Don't cram too much into each scene
  • Use Captions: Many people watch videos without sound
  • Brand Consistency: Add your logo and use school colors
  • Call to Action: Always end with a clear next step
  • Test on Mobile: Most viewers will watch on phones

YouTube (Horizontal)

  • Use 16:9 aspect ratio
  • 2-5 minutes acceptable
  • Include keyword-rich title
  • Add description and tags
