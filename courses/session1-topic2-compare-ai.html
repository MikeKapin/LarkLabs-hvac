üéØ Hands-On Exercise: The Email Challenge

Test all three AI assistants with the same task and compare their responses.

Task: Write a Professional Email

Scenario: You need to email department heads about upcoming professional development sessions.

Prompt to use with each AI:

Write a professional email to department heads announcing a new AI tools professional development workshop. Include: - Workshop date: February 15, 2025 - Time: 2:00-4:00 PM - Location: Conference Room B - Topics: ChatGPT for productivity, Gamma for presentations - RSVP deadline: February 8 - Keep it under 150 words

üìù What to Notice
