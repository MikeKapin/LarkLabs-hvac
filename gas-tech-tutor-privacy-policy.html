Privacy Policy - Gas Tech Tutor

Effective Date: September 25, 2025

1. Introduction

LARK Labs ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Gas Tech Tutor mobile application ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Study progress and quiz results (stored locally on your device)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Feedback and communications you send to us

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage analytics (features used, time spent in app)
  • Crash reports and error logs for app improvement

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contact list or personal files

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Gas Tech Tutor app functionality
  • Track your study progress and quiz performance
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Analyze app usage to enhance educational content

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Study progress and quiz results are stored locally on your device
  • Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect your information
  • Data Retention: App usage data is retained for analytical purposes to improve the educational experience

5. Third-Party Services

The Gas Tech Tutor app may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics and app performance monitoring
  • Crash reporting and bug detection

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

The Gas Tech Tutor app is designed for gas technician training and certification preparation. While not specifically designed for children under 13, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your locally stored study data
  • Delete the app and all associated local data
  • Contact us with questions about your data
  • Opt out of analytics by adjusting your device settings

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or by posting the updated policy on our website. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
