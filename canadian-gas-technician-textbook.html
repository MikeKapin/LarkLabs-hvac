About This Textbook

The Canadian Gas Technician Textbook is a comprehensive resource designed for students pursuing G2 and G3 certification through the Technical Standards and Safety Authority (TSSA) in Ontario, Canada.

This textbook covers all essential topics required for gas technician certification, including gas properties, piping systems, appliances, venting, electrical fundamentals, troubleshooting, and safety procedures - all aligned with CSA B149.1 and B149.2 standards.

Whether you're a student, apprentice, or experienced technician looking to upgrade your certification, this textbook provides the knowledge foundation you need to succeed.
